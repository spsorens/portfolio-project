About Shannan

My journey in web development is new and somewhat unexpected. I've been working in the insurance industry for the past 8 years. I developed an interest in web development after spending the better part of the past year learning the dev side of things in my role as a medical underwriter. Intrigued, I decided to learn how to code in my spare time so I could understand more about the nuts and bolts of the project. Thanks to SheCodes, I've been able to dive in. Throughout this journey, I've learned a lot and unlocked a new passion and plan on continuing on this journey.
